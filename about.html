What We Do

Course Flex is a new and revolutionized course finder catered for students at the University Of Toronto, St. George Campus. Akin to the traditional course finder, Course Flex provides a multitude of ways for students to explore hundreds of amazing courses this school has to offer!

How We're Different

Our goal is to incorporate a more flexible and diverse search engine with a user friendly interface. A major problem with the current course finder web application is that the search is limited to very few filters. For example, you can only search per program, therefore we want to remove this restriction and expand the search criteria. Some new filters we plan on adding are time slot, term, professor, POSt and so on. In addition to this, a new component we will be implementing is the ability to create a personal account where students can keep track of courses that have grasped their interest.

How It Works

Every time a student shows interest in a course, it will be recorded as a ”flex” so that others can view the popularity of that course. A 5 star rating system will also be an option for every course. Students that have taken that particular course can rate the course based on different components such as workload, difficulty and professor. With this information, we can widen our collection of filter options. For instance, searching courses with a workload rating of 3 stars or less.
